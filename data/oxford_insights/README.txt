NOTES ON 2024 DATA UNAVAILABILITY
----------------------------------
The Oxford Insights website now gates all data downloads behind a Gravity Forms
contact form requiring: name, email, organization, organization type, and country.
Downloads are then provided via email or form confirmation page (JS-loaded),
making direct URL access impossible without form submission and credential entry.

Extensive URL pattern searching was attempted using known naming conventions
from other years. No matching URL was found for 2024 indicator data.

Possible alternative: Submit the form at
https://oxfordinsights.com/ai-readiness/government-ai-readiness-index-2025/
with your organization details to receive download links.

COLUMN STRUCTURE (All Years)
------------------------------
Three-pillar structure (scores 0-100):
  1. GOVERNMENT PILLAR
     - Vision (national AI strategy, commitment)
     - Governance & Ethics (regulation, oversight)
     - Digital Capacity (e-government, digital services)
     - Adaptability & Innovation (government innovation capacity)

  2. TECHNOLOGY SECTOR PILLAR
     - Data Availability (open data, data portals)
     - Data Representativeness (data coverage)
     - Data Infrastructure (broadband, connectivity)

  3. DATA & INFRASTRUCTURE PILLAR
     - AI Research output
     - Tech sector capacity
     - Human capital (STEM graduates, developers)
